The OG Recipe For The Best Ptitim

1. Cut an onion into cubes.

2. Heat two tablespoons of olive oil.

3. Sauté the onion until translucent.

4. Add 1 cup of ptitim and toast until golden brown.

5. Pour in 1.5 cups of boiling water.

6. Add 1 tablespoon of chicken broth powder.

7. Cover and cook on medium heat.

8. Once the water evaporates, turn off the heat and let it rest.

9. Once it rested take a bowl and serve.
